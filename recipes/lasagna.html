Classic Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna layers rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese between sheets of pasta. It's comforting, satisfying, and perfect for feeding a crowd. Make it ahead and bake when ready.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 egg
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown ground beef in a skillet, drain excess fat, then stir in marinara sauce and simmer.
  3. Mix ricotta with egg, half the Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish. Repeat and finish with remaining cheese.
  5. Bake covered for 25 minutes, then uncovered for 10–15 minutes until bubbly. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
